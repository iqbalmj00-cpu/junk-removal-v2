The Clean-Sweep Process

Electronic waste requires specialized handling. Our process ensures data privacy and maximizes material recovery, keeping toxins out of the soil.

Collection & Sort

We collect your IT assets directly from your facility using secure transport. Items are cataloged and initially sorted by device type (monitors, towers, peripherals).

Data Destruction

Critical Step: All storage media (HDDs, SSDs) are removed. Data is wiped using DoD standards or physically shredded. We provide Certificates of Destruction.

Devices are disassembled. Metals, plastics, and glass are separated and sent to specialized downstream partners for raw material manufacturing.
